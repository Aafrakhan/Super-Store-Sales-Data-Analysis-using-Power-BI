# Super Store Sales Data Analysis using Power BI

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Super Store's sales and profit performance across regions, categories, and shipping modes. The project includes a main sales dashboard for historical performance tracking and a forecasting page projecting sales trends 15 days into the future, broken down by state.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power BI Forecasting (Analytics pane)
- Slicers (Region: Central, East, South, West)

## Dashboard Features
### Main Dashboard
- **KPI Cards** – Total Orders, Total Sales, Total Profit, Average Ship Days
- **Sales by Payment Mode** – COD, Online, and Card payment distribution
- **Sales by Region** – Breakdown across Central, East, South, and West
- **Sales by Segment** – Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office share
- **Sales & Profit by Month** – Year-over-year monthly trend (2019 vs 2020)
- **Sales by Ship Mode** – Standard, Second, First, and Same Day shipping
- **Sales by Category & Sub-Category** – Performance across Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture, and sub-items like Phones, Chairs, Binders
- **Profit and Sales by State** – Geographic map visualization

### Forecast Dashboard
- **Sales Forecast (15 Days)** – Predictive trend line with confidence interval
- **Sales by State** – State-wise sales ranking (California, New York, Texas, etc.)

## Key Insights
- Total sales reached 1.6M with a total profit of 175K across 22K orders
- Average shipping time was 4 days
- Cash on Delivery (COD) led payment modes at 43%, followed by Online (35%) and Cards (22%)
- West region contributed the highest share of sales at 33%
- Consumer segment accounted for nearly half of all sales (48%)
- Office Supplies was the top-performing category with 0.64M in sales
- Sales showed a strong upward trend toward the end of 2020, with the forecast projecting continued growth into early 2021
- California led all states in sales at 0.34M, followed by New York (0.19M) and Texas (0.12M)
